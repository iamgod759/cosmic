How it fits together

Two planes, many layers, one platform.

Everything in VMware splits into a control plane (the brains that decide and configure — vCenter, NSX Manager, the Avi Controller) and a data plane (the muscle that moves packets and runs workloads — ESX hosts, Service Engines, Edge nodes). Learn that one idea and the whole stack clicks.
